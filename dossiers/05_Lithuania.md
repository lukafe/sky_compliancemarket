## 2. Regime detail

- **Legacy VCEO regime** had low capital and KYC bar — many shell registrations.
- **CASP authorization** requires meaningful capital (€50k–€150k), AML program, custody segregation, governance.
- **BoL has signalled** most legacy VCEOs will not migrate — winddown or sale to authorized CASPs.
- **Travel Rule:** €0 threshold.
