# Dossier — France 🇫🇷

## 0. AI Executive Summary

The **AMF + ACPR** are running the **PSAN → CASP transition**: France's pre-MiCA PSAN (registered or licensed) regime sunsets, all entities must be **MiCA-CASP authorized by 30 Jun 2026** (with national extension up to 18 months for some PSAN-registered firms). France has the strongest national pre-MiCA crypto register (~110 PSAN registrations), so the migration is the largest single-country in-scope population in MiCA. CertiK wins on **smart-contract + Travel Rule wiring + custody attestation**, particularly for the long tail of PSAN-registered (not licensed) firms that must now meet the higher CASP bar. Confidence HIGH on regime, MEDIUM on enforcement intensity.
