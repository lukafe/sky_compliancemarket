## 2. Regime detail

### 2.1 Licensing requirements
- **Scope:** All crypto asset trading platforms, custody services, wallet providers serving Turkish residents.
- **Capital:** Minimum paid-in capital significantly raised (~TRY 150M for platforms per draft; final figures in 2025 secondary reg).
- **Custody segregation:** Mandatory — customer assets separated from operating capital; periodic attestations.
- **AML/CFT (MASAK):** Travel Rule operational; KYC tiers; STR pipeline.
- **Stablecoin:** Treated within general crypto framework; specific issuance rules expected post-MR.

### 2.2 What changes by 2026-03-31
- Platforms not authorized cannot serve Turkish residents.
- Foreign exchanges (Binance, Kraken, etc.) must register or be blocked.
- Custody attestation cycles begin.

### 2.3 Enforcement track record (rolling 24mo)
- 2024–2025: SPK published platform blacklist; ~10 unregistered foreign platforms restricted.
- MASAK fines: low-volume but increasing; biggest enforcement risk is **post-deadline** crackdown.
