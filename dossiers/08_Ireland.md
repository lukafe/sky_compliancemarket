## 0. AI Executive Summary

The **Central Bank of Ireland (CBI)** is supervising the **MiCA → CASP transition** ending **30 Jun 2026**. Ireland is a strategic CASP location for **non-EU originators** (Coinbase EU, Gemini EU, Kraken EU, Ripple, others) seeking MiCA passporting.
