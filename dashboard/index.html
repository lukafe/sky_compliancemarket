Two-axis composite — orthogonal, never summed

Axis 1 — Attractiveness (weighted arithmetic + BAN GATE): Adoption (30%) + AML maturity (20%) + Enforcement (20%) + Volume tier (20%) + Institutional depth (10%).

Axis 2 — Urgency (geometric of HARD CORE + additive SOFT + DEADLINE GATE):
• Hard core 85%: Deadline proximity (40%) + Lifecycle (30%) + Trigger (30%)
• Soft 15% (capped): Non-compliance gap (50%) + Demand momentum (50%)

Gates. BAN GATE caps Attractiveness at 25 for total-ban jurisdictions (prevents grey-market inflation). DEADLINE GATE caps Urgency at 30 when no dated trigger exists.

Why geometric on hard core. Non-compensatory: urgency requires deadline AND lifecycle AND trigger together, not just one strong leg.
