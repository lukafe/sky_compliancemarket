Two-axis composite

Orthogonal axes, never summed. Quadrant placement is the decision frame.

Axis 1 — Attractiveness (weighted arithmetic mean + BAN GATE)
Adoption (30%) + AML maturity (20%) + Enforcement (20%) + Volume tier (20%) + Institutional depth (10%, SYNTH-capped)

Axis 2 — Urgency (geometric mean of HARD CORE + additive SOFT + DEADLINE GATE)
Hard core (85%): Deadline proximity (40%) + Lifecycle (30%) + Trigger (30%)
Soft (15%, capped): Non-compliance gap (50%) + Demand momentum (50%)

Gates. BAN GATE caps Attractiveness at 25 for total-ban jurisdictions. DEADLINE GATE caps Urgency at 30 when no dated trigger exists.

Geometric mean of hard core = non-compensatory: urgency needs deadline AND lifecycle AND trigger together, not just one.

Refresh policy (honestly scoped)

Per spec §I — Phase 7 honesty rule.

Auto-refresh (weekly):
• Soft signals (non-compliance gap, demand momentum)
• Rescoring of existing inputs

Manual refresh (quarterly + on-event):
• HARD legal facts — deadline, framework citation, regulator, thresholds, tax
• Each updated field re-cites source + as-of date + confidence

No HARD field is ever auto-imputed. UNVERIFIED is allowed and tracked — never inferred.
